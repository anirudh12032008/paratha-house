Our Story

From ₹2,600 and a
rolling pin, in 2005.

Hemant Sahu left Bhopal for Bengaluru in 2005 carrying ₹2,600, a borrowed rolling pin and his mother's paratha recipes. He set up a small stall on Old Airport Road and began feeding working professionals who missed home-cooked food.

Word spread the way good food does — quietly and unstoppably. In 2022 he opened a second outlet opposite Embassy Golf Links in Domlur. Same recipes, same ghee, same conviction: honest food at prices anyone can afford, ₹100–150 for a full paratha meal.
